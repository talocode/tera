## Music

- Use royalty-free ambient track
- Volume: 15-20% (barely audible)
- Consistent throughout, no drops or swells
- Suggestion: search "lo-fi study beat" or "ambient focus" on YouTube Audio Library

## Editing Notes

- Every text fade: 0.3s in, 0.3s out
- Every prompt: type at natural speed (not sped up)
- Every Tera response: pause 2s after it appears
- Transitions: simple cuts, no fancy effects
- Total runtime: ~9 minutes (same as voiceover version)

## Text Style Guide

- Font: Inter (or similar sans-serif)
- Main text: #FFFFFF (white)
- Accent text: #00FF88 (neon green)
- Background: #0A0A0A (near black) for step titles
- Position: centered for step titles, bottom for callouts
- Fade in: 0.3s
- Fade out: 0.3s
