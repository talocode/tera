## Quick Start: Record in 30 Minutes

1. **Open browser** — zoom 110%, bookmarks hidden, notifications off
2. **Open teraai.chat** — log in, clear chat history
3. **Open prompts file** — `prompts.txt`
4. **Start screen recording** — 1920x1080, 30fps
5. **Record Section 1-2** (hook + problem) — 2 minutes
6. **Record Section 3** (Step 1: understand) — 2 minutes
7. **Record Section 4** (Step 2: test) — 2 minutes
8. **Record Section 5** (Step 3: plan) — 2 minutes
9. **Record Section 6** (Step 4: summarize) — 2 minutes
10. **Record Section 7-8** (assessment + CTA) — 2 minutes
11. **Stop recording** — you now have 14 minutes of raw footage
12. **Edit** — trim to 9 minutes, add text overlays, export

**Total time: ~30 minutes recording + 2-3 hours editing**
