## 9. TeraAI Video Optimization Rules

### One Problem Per Video

Every TeraAI video must target one clear viewer problem:

| Problem | Example video |
|---------|--------------|
| I don't understand this topic. | "How to Study with AI" |
| I forget what I read. | Active recall + quiz methods |
| I don't know how to study. | Study plan creation |
| I need practice questions. | AI quiz generation |
| I need a study plan. | 7-day plan walkthrough |
| I need help writing. | Essay writing with AI |
| I need help researching. | Research mode demo |
| I need help coding. | Code review demo |

### One Clear Result

By the end, the viewer knows exactly how to use AI to study better. No ambiguity. No filler.

### Content Mix (30-day cadence)

| Format | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Long-form | 1 per week | Search, watch hours, depth |
| Shorts | 3–5 per week | Discovery, subscribers, reach |
| Community post | 1 per week (when available) | Engagement, retention |

**Note:** Shorts watch hours from the Shorts Feed do NOT count toward the 4,000 long-form watch-hour threshold. Don't rely on Shorts alone for monetization.

### Quality Gate

Internal target before publishing:

| Metric | Minimum | Ideal |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Publish score | 75/100 | 80+/100 |
| Hook score | 10/15+ | 12/15+ |
| Demo usefulness | 10/15+ | 12/15+ |
